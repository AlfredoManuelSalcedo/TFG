Understanding English Tenses

Past

The Past Simple tense is used to describe actions that happened at a specific time in the past.

Structure:

Subject + past form of the verb

Examples:

- I walked to school yesterday.

- She loved the movie.

- They played soccer last week.

Past Continuous

The Past Continuous tense is used to describe actions that were in progress at a specific time in the past.

Structure:

Subject + was/were + verb-ing

Examples:

- I was reading a book when you called.

- She was studying all night.

- They were playing soccer at 3 PM yesterday.
